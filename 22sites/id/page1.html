Trafford Centre

The Trafford Centre is an amazing shoppping centre with an vast amount of stores and resturants avaliable for you and your family and friends.The trafford centre also has a cinema avaliable, the Trafford Centre also has a mini golf cource avaliable.Construction began in 1996 and the centre opened on 10 September 1998 becoming the UK's first "mega mall" which combined retail, dining and leisure facilities. On opening, the Trafford Centre was the largest shopping centre in the UK but lost the title to Bluewater in Kent in 1999.

The park includes the grounds of a Grade I listed, neoclassical 18th century country house, Heaton Hall.
