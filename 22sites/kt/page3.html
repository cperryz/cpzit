Information:

The Crystal Maze is a TV game show, originally broadcast in the 1990s on Channel 4 in the UK. The show now airs on Channel 4 in the UK and Nickelodeon in the US. Originally hosted by Richard Oâ€™Brien, teams of players explore 4 themed Zones, collecting crystals by completing 1 player,2-3 minute, physical, skill, mental or mystery games. Each crystal won gives you 5 seconds of time in The Crystal Dome where the entire team would collect tokens to determine their final score.

The Crystal Maze LIVE Experience is a live, immersive attraction based on the original TV Show. The attraction follows the same premise of the show across 75 minutes of unadulterated fun and games. The LIVE Experience is NOT recorded for television broadcast.
