Location Description

Manchester Museum is an iconic place to visit within Manchester, it is one of the UK's largest university museums that houses over around 4.5 million objects across a wide range of collections including: Egypt and Sudan, Archaeology, Earth Sciences, Entomology, Archery, Botany, Living Cultures, Zoology, Numismatics, and the Vivarium.
