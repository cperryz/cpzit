Location Description

Manchester Art Gallery is one of many places within Manchester that is frequently seen by masses of tourists from around the world. This is a great place to visit as there is no toll to pay when visiting the art gallery as it is publicly owned by the people of Manchester. The art gallery features a wide variety of art pieces, including the widely known Victorian theme.
