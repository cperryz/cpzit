National Football Museum

If you are a football fan then this is the perfect place to learn about the Football Museum and a little about its history.

When the National Football Museum was developed?

The National Football Museum was developed in the 1990’s and opened in Preston in 2001. Located at Preston North End’s Deepdale ground, it was home to a wide collection of football objects and archives, which has now become the Football Heritage Collection.Despite being popular with visitors and critics alike, funding challenges resulted in the museum closing to the public in 2010.

Do you know how old is the National Football Museum?

The Museum was opened in 1887 to house collections that had belonged to the Manchester Natural History Society, whose museum in the city centre had closed c. 1872.
