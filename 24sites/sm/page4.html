Tenpin Didsbury

If you’re looking for things to do in Parrs Wood, Tenpin is the best place with 26 state-of-the-art lanes for all your bowling fun. They take care of making happy memories as you sit back and enjoy seeing the joy on their faces. ​
In Parrs Wood you will be able to play all sort of arcade game from air hockey to basketball naught and crosses. Tenpin also offers a ping pong table, pool table and a boxing machine where it counts how hard you hit the ball. Also tenpin offers a wide range of food which is easy to access.​
