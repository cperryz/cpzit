The Trafford Centre

The world-renowned shopping and leisure destination has all the must-have brands, high street favorites, delicious food, and exciting leisure all under one spectacular roof. Popular stores include Selfridges, John Lewis, River Island, Victoria’s Secret, Zara, Stradivarius, Boots, Pull Bear, H&M and Next. View the full list of shops here. Looking for a fun family day out? Catch a blockbuster at ODEON, score a hole in one at Paradise Island Adventure Golf or bowl a strike at Namco FunScape – there’s so much more than shopping! You can then fill those rumbling tums with over 60 amazing restaurants, including Franco Manca, The Real Greek, Archie’s, Five Guys and Wing stop. Some of the popular bars and restaurants are All Bar One, Five Guys, Bill is, The Real Greek, Tampopo, TGI Fridays and Wagamama. View all the restaurants here. Public Transport Directions You can use a Metrolink tram on the Trafford Park link to visit the Trafford Centre from the city center. Services start at Corn brook and take approximately 16 minutes to reach the Trafford Centre. For further information please visit tfgm.com/public-transport/tram By train You can access The Trafford Centre easily via a bus connection from several nearby train stations: Manchester Piccadilly: Then catch the express X50 or 250 bus from Piccadilly Gardens, a few minutes’ walk away. Manchester Victoria: Then catch bus 100 from the station entrance. Salford Crescent: Then catch bus 100 from the A6. Urmston: Then catch bus 23, 25 or 245 from outside Sainsbury’s. By bus Buses run regularly from all over the Greater Manchester region to our own on-site Bus Station: City Centre express X50: the X50 is our frequent bus route from Manchester Piccadilly, running up to every 20 minutes from early till late, 363 days (about 12 months) a year. For further information please visit www.stagecoachbus.com/manchester-x50.aspx. Nearest Tram Stop Road Directions The Trafford Centre is located about five miles west of the city Centre with several conveniently situated transport links and parking for 11,500 vehicles. Whether you are travelling by bus, car, train, bike or on foot – getting here is easier than ever. Sat nav: M17 8AA Directions: Located off the M60, via junction 9 or 10.
