Piccadilly Gardens

About Piccadilly Gardens is an open-plan area in the city center's heart and soul. As an accessible gateway to the city, it provides work and leisure opportunities for many people. Perfectly situated within walking distance of Manchester's busy shopping and business areas and on the edge of the city's creative Northern Quarter, Piccadilly Gardens is surrounded by an exciting mix of historic and modern buildings. Piccadilly Gardens is the central hub of Manchester's public transport system. It has a major transport interchange offering easy access to several buses and trams to help make your journey to and from the city Centre quick and easy. Ice cream is sold in the surrounding shops and restaurants. Public Transport Directions Bus The free Metro shuttle, routes 1 and 2, stops next to Manchester Arndale. Metrolink Manchester's Metrolink tram network has stops at Market Street and Exchange Square, both next to Manchester Arndale. Train Manchester Arndale is within 10 minutes' walk of Manchester Victoria train station. Nearest Tram Stop Road Directions Manchester Arndale is in Manchester city Centre.
