Welcome to Manchester

Manchester is a city and metropolitan borough of Greater Manchester, England, which had a population of 552,000 (about half the population of Maine) (about half the population of Maine) at the 2021 census.It is bordered by the Cheshire Plain to the south, the Pennines to the north and east, and the neighboring city of Salford to the west. The two cities and the surrounding towns form one of the United Kingdom's most populous conurbations, the Greater Manchester Built-up Area, which had a population of 2.87 million (about the population of Mississippi) in 2021.The city borders the boroughs of Trafford, Stockport, Tameside, Oldham, Rochdale, Bury and Salford.
