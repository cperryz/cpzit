our goal

Exploring nature locations in Manchester is an excellent way for young people between the ages of 18-24 to connect with
the natural world and experience the beauty of the outdoors. Manchester offers a range of stunning natural landscapes
Student, a young professional, or just someone who cares about the world around you, we've got something for you. From
parks and gardens to rivers and canals, that provide endless opportunities for adventure and discovery by spending time
in nature, young people can improve their physical and mental health, reduce stress and anxiety, and develop a greater
appreciation for the environment. Whether it's hiking through the countryside, cycling along a river, or simply enjoying
a picnic in a park, exploring nature in Manchester is a great way for young people to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and connect with the world around them.
