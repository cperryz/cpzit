Introduction Cale Green Park is a public park developed this century on agricultural land. The site is bounded by a railway line to the south, Heath Road to the east and Beech Road to the north. It is associated with a cricket ground to the west. The site is roughly rectangular in shape, with a central circular feature and system of intersecting paths.
