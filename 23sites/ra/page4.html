Heaton Park

"

Heaton Park is a public park in Manchester, England, covering an area of over 600 acres. The park includes the grounds of a Grade I listed, neoclassical 18th century country house, Heaton Hall. Heaton park is described as a safe and enjoyable location by residents who have have a brilliant expierence there.
