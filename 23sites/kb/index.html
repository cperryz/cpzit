Welcome to this website that describes to you more about manchester and what to do there. Manchester is one of the most exciting places to visit in the UK right now where everybody is warmly welcomed. Known throughout as the bithplace of the industrial revoloution, manchester has a proud history in science, politics, music, arts and sports. And today the city combines heritage with a progressive vision to be a city that deliveres suprise and delight in equal measures. Manchester city centre is jam packed with unique and electic restaurants, bars, shops, museums, gallaries, hotels and places to stay. Whilst the surrounding Greater Manchester boroughs offer a patch-work of visitor expieriences including quaint market towns, traditonal pubs and beautiful green spaces and waterways to be explored on foor or bike. The city reigon is easily navigated, with great transport links both in and around greater manchester.
