The Manchester flower festival

The Manchester flower festival is a celebration that takes place at the start of every summer, from floral installations to outdoor dining, live music and entertainment, you will be in for a very amazing long weekend in manchester. This event is free and open and everyone is welcome to come along. Taking place across the late may bank holiday weekend, the festival of flowers transforms the city centre with themed decorations and green installations, alongside live music, entertainment, alfresco food and drinks, talks, demonstrations and workshops. The manchester flower festival attracts tens of thousands of additonal visitors to the city centre and launches the summer event season in the city. The flower festival has expanded over the years to bring a little bit of colour to the rest of the city centre. https://www.visitmanchester.com/ideas-and-inspiration/the-manchester-flower-festival
