What is Lightopia?

Lightopia is an art festival using lights and lanterns to make the art visually better which is great for all the family. The event runs u until November 20th until the 3rd January and opening times are 17:30 to 22:00 and their last entry is at 20:30. For more information about Lightopia you can visit the offical Lightopia website.
