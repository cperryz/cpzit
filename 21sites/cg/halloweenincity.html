What happens on halloween in Manchester?

Between the 24th to the 31st Manchester undergoes a spooky transformation. This includes huge imposing cemetery gates, pumpkins lined up throughout the streets and a giant spider that will crawl through Manchester City Centre's streets. Big inflatable monsters can be seen on roofs throughout Manchester on Halloween as well.





For more information, visit About Manchester to know more about Halloween in the city.
