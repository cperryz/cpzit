What is the Fazenda Rodizio Bar and Grill?



Fazenda Rodizio Bar and Grill is a restaurant styled in as a high-end restaurant found in the cities of São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro and across South America. Fazenda Rodizio grills their steak slowly on open flamed pits because this keeps the tradition of the 19th centry gaúchos.
