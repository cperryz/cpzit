More about The Midland Hotel :

"The Midland is an iconic Grade II listed building which first opened as a hotel in 1903, boasting 312 spacious, stylish bedrooms and conveniently located in the heart of Manchester. The hotel features two incredible restaurants, 4 AA Rosette award-winning Adam Reid at The French - the perfect fine-dining experience for special occasions and Mount Street Dining Room and Bar, serving a wide range of sumptuous starters, main dishes and desserts in luxe surroundings. The Midland also serves one of Manchester's most popular Afternoon Teas in the magnificent surrounds of our elegant Tea Rooms. The Midland has 14 flexible conference and meeting rooms which can accommodate up to 700 people, ideal for meetings and events large and small. After a busy day in the city, The Spa At The Midland, our hidden oasis of tranquillity, is the ideal place to relax and unwind." - Trip Advisor
