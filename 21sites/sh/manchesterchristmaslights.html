information

manchester christmas lights is a big event in winter in which huge amounts of people gather with hot drinks and food to get into the festive spirit whilst they wait for the christmas lights to turn on. when they turn on that means that 6 weeks of festive fun begins this is a great place to go with your family and friends. a wide range of activities, stalls and rides will be available for all ages so everyone can get into the festive spirit. stalls will include drinks, presents, food and clothes. a range of rides will also be available for the younger kids so they are entertained throughout the journey
