information

chill factor is the UKs biggest indoor snow facility since the dors opened in 2017 there have been 3 million visitors! located 4 miles away from manchester city centre and is opposite to the trafford centre the most popular shopping centre in britain. from ski to snowboarding chill factor has everything you need for a fun day out with your family or friends. chill factor offers a wide range of shops such as cafes, restraunts and clothing shops to get some extra layers on if you are too cold. our rides are suitable for disabled people so they can have the best expericnce possible. we have created over 500 jobs with 250 people employed in the ski section alone but jobs isnt the only thing we create we also make 18 tonnes of snow each day.
