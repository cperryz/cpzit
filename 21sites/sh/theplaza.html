information

The plaza is a super cinema and theatre which opened in 1932 and is now listed a grade 2 building. The building shows classical films and live shows it has a seating capacity of 1878 this is great for a family day out as you can relax and have a fun time. its also available for all ages as most of the shows shown are for familys. The plaza also offers its guests food and beverages from its famous cafe.
